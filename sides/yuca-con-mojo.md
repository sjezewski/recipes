# Yuca Con Mojo #

## Ingredients ##

- 1 big yucca root
- 1 onion chopped fine (red shallot is best)
- 6 garlic cloves (pressed)
- 4 limes juiced
- 1 lemon juiced

## Directions ##

- Boil yucca in covered pot ~20 min (add salt to boil), then drain
- Heat garlic / salt / olive oil / lemon & lime juice / onion
- Add yucca and lightly brown
