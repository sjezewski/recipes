# Bourbon Onions

## Ingredients

- 1 shot bulleit bourbon
- 2 onions
- 1 tsp chipotle powder


## Instructions

- cut the onions course
- fry em up
  - high heat initially w the bourbon
  - then medium to carmelize / reduce

## Modifications

- Use tequila instead of bourbon
- Add meyer lemon
- And a bit of cumin / coriander / garlic
